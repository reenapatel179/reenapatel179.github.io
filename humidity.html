Humidity

Humidity is the amount of water vapor in the air. Most atmospheric water vapor comes from evaporation of water from the ocean or other bodies of water. Water vapor in the air greatly affects the type of weather we have. It is the source of all clouds, fog, and precipitation. Water vapor also helps warm the air when it absorbs energy from the sun.
